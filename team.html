ABOUT Codeasaurous

The Codeasaurous Team came to be thru code.org. Originally starting as just the owner and one other, the team has grown greatly ever since. Many amazing projects have been put out by our team including but not limited to discord bots, websites, code.org projects, apps, and much more. Many who have joined and or been a part of the community say that they learned heaps of information so quickly!
